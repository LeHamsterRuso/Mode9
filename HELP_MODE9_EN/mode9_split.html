MODE9_SPLIT()

MODE9_SPLIT( <slot> , <camera> , <OFFSET m9.z> , <x> , <y> , <width> , <height> )

Description:

Defines a viewport for SPLIT-SCREEN: up to 4 simultaneous cameras, each with its own m9 structure and its own rectangle on screen. Each slot is an independent view with its own camera.

For billboards to appear in all views, the FPG must be loaded into the DLL with MODE9_LOADFPG().

Parameters:

ParameterMeaning
slotViewport number (0..3).
cameraId of the camera process for this view.
OFFSET m9.zAddress of this camera's m9 structure (each view has its own).
x, yTop-left corner of the viewport, in pixels.
width, heightSize of the viewport in pixels.

Returns: 1.

Example program: 
// Two players, horizontal split-screen (320x200)
MODE9_LOADFPG("FPG/MODE9.FPG");
MODE9_SPLIT(0, jug1, OFFSET m9a.z, 0,   0, 320, 100);  // top
MODE9_SPLIT(1, jug2, OFFSET m9b.z, 0, 100, 320, 100);  // bottom
See: MODE9_REGION() - MODE9_LOADFPG() - START_MODE9()
