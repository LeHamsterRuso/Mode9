MODE9_FOV()

MODE9_FOV( <degrees> )

Description:

Adjusts the "viewing angle": how much of the world the camera captures from side to side, like the zoom on a photo camera. This is what 3D games call FOV (field of view). The default value (about 71 degrees) gives a natural, balanced view.

Lowering the angle (e.g. 60) is like zooming in: you see less to the sides but everything looks "closer". Raising it (90 or 110) gives a wide-angle effect: you see much more around you with a greater sense of speed, though objects at the edges look slightly stretched.

Parameters:

ParameterMeaning
degreesHorizontal field of view: 60 = narrow (zoom), 71 = natural (default), 90 = wide, 110 = very wide (wide-angle).

Returns: 0.

Example program: 
MODE9_FOV(90);   // wide-angle view
See: MODE9_SET()
