SKY9_OFF()

SKY9_OFF()

Description:

Deactivates the panoramic sky and reverts to the flat background colour set by SET_ENV_COLOR9(). Useful when transitioning from an exterior (with sky) to an interior (without sky).

Returns: 0.

Example program: 
// When entering the castle interior: remove the sky and darken the background
LOAD_WLD9("CASTIN.OBJ", "MAP/CASTIN.MAP", 100);
SKY9_OFF();
SET_ENV_COLOR9(8, 8, 12);   // dark interior atmosphere
See: LOAD_SKY9() - SET_ENV_COLOR9()
