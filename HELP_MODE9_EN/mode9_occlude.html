MODE9_OCCLUDE()

MODE9_OCCLUDE( <on> )

Description:

Decides whether sprites are hidden when a wall (or another part of the scene) is in front of them. With the option enabled (default), an enemy behind a column is occluded, as expected. If you disable it, sprites are always drawn on top of everything, even when they should be hidden.

Disabling it is useful for things you always want visible, such as an objective icon or a map marker, regardless of intervening walls.

Parameters:

ParameterMeaning
on1 = sprites are occluded by the scene, as expected (default); 0 = sprites are always visible, drawn on top of everything.

Returns: 0.

Example program: 
MODE9_OCCLUDE(1);   // enemy behind a column is hidden (default)
MODE9_OCCLUDE(0);   // markers/icons always visible
See: MODE9_BILLBOARD() - MODE9_PROJECT()
