MODE9_FOG_OFF()

MODE9_FOG_OFF()

Description:

Deactivates the fog enabled by SET_FOG9(). Geometry is drawn at full sharpness at any distance.

Returns: 0.

Example program: 
MODE9_FOG_OFF();   // remove the fog
See: SET_FOG9()
